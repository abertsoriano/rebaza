Tax Services

While providing legal counsel, Rebaza, Alcázar & De Las Casas Abogados approach tax and fiscal considerations unique to each transaction, resulting in optimum cost structures.

In this regard, comprehensive advice to our clients takes into consideration tax components that may sometimes be critical in determining the structure of each transaction.

Our vast experience in Mergers and Acquisitions, and Corporate Finance allows lawyers at Rebaza, Alcázar & De Las Casas to remain alert to the tax implications and risks related to financial transactions so as to properly assess those potential impacts—together with our clients and their financial advisors.
