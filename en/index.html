Recent Experience:

We advised AmBev S.A in closing the sale of the Peruvian brewery as part of its global integration with SAB Miller.

W.W.G. Of Peru S.A., in partnership with the Spanish company Quirónsalud, leader in the provision of health services in that country, acquired 50% of CRP Holdco S.A., owner of 95.5% of Clínica Administradora Ricardo Palma
